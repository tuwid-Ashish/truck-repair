Our Services

Engine Repairs & Rebuilds

From full in-frame rebuilds to complex diagnostics, we’ve got the tools, expertise, and experience to get your engine running like new. We use high-quality parts and follow precise manufacturer specifications to ensure lasting performance and reliability.

Our services cover overhauls, injector service, turbo repairs, EGR/DPF solutions, and driveline components all tuned to OEM standards. We tackle power loss, smoke, knocking, and fuel efficiency issues using live data diagnostics and load testing.

Scope & Issues We Handle

  • Value Tune up
  • Overhauls
  • Injector service
  • Turbo repairs
  • EGR/DPF solutions
  • Driveline components (OEM standards)
  • Power loss
  • Smoke
  • Knocking
  • Poor fuel efficiency
  • Low boost
  • Misfire
  • Injector leakback
  • Overheating

Diagnosis via live data and load testing.

From rebuilds and tuning to component replacements, we restore power and reliability where it matters most under the hood.

Electrical & Diagnostics

Advanced scanning, wiring repairs, sensor testing, and module programming to resolve intermittent faults.

We use dealer-level diagnostic tools and up-to-date software to ensure your truck meets current emissions standards and avoid costly downtime. Whether you’re dealing with DPF, SCR, EGR systems or a Check Engine light related to emissions, we’ll pinpoint the issue and fix it right.

Scope & Issues We Handle

  • Advanced ECU scanning
  • Wiring repairs & harness restoration
  • Sensor testing & replacement
  • Module programming & coding
  • DPF / SCR / EGR diagnosis & service
  • Emissions-related “Check Engine” resolution
  • No-start / hard-start
  • DTCs (diagnostic trouble codes)
  • CAN bus / communication faults
  • Parasitic battery drain

Diagnosis via OEM/dealer-level tools, guided fault trees, oscilloscope traces, and live data.

Cooling & HVAC Systems

Service and repair for radiators, water pumps, thermostats, fan clutches, heater cores, charge-air coolers, and A/C components to keep temps stable under load.

We quickly isolate overheating, coolant loss, weak heat, and poor A/C performance to protect uptime and fuel efficiency.

Scope & Issues We Handle

  • Radiators, pumps & thermostats
  • Fan clutches, belts & tensioners
  • Hose & surge-tank leak repairs
  • CAC pressure tests & leak fixes
  • Heater cores & control valves
  • A/C compressor, condenser, dryer, TXV
  • Overheating, coolant loss, low heat/no A/C
  • Oil–coolant mix / EGR-cooler suspicion

Diagnosis via pressure & UV-dye tests, block tests, IR temp mapping, fan-command checks, and A/C vacuum/recharge.

Welding & Fabrication

We work on steel, stainless, and aluminum using MIG, TIG, and stick (arc) processes.

We repair cracks, replace damaged sections, and fabricate brackets and mounts so your equipment is safe and ready for work.

Scope & Issues We Handle

  • Crossmembers, frame brackets, spring hangers, and gussets
  • Landing gear, kingpin plate surrounds, and dolly repairs
  • Aluminum boxes, steps, tanks, doors, and hinges
  • Stake pockets, rub rails, bumpers, and headache rack work
  • Cracked welds, torn mounts, and broken tabs
  • Custom brackets, tool mounts, exhaust and heat shield fixes
  • On-site welding for roadside or yard repairs when safe

Prep includes cutting, beveling, fit-up, and proper shielding. We finish with cleanup and paint where required.

Brakes & Suspension

Service and repair for air brakes and chassis so your truck or trailer stops straight and rides stable under load.

We correct pulls, vibrations, and air leaks, set proper brake stroke and ride height, and verify ABS operation for safe, compliant performance.

Scope & Issues We Handle

  • Air brake diagnosis, chambers, valves, lines, and dryers
  • Slack adjuster setup and stroke measurement
  • Pads, rotors, drums, shoes, S cam parts, and hardware
  • ABS wheel speed sensors and wiring faults
  • Wheel bearings and hub seals
  • Leaf springs, U bolts, bushings, and shackles
  • Airbags, height control valves, and shocks
  • Steering and kingpin play checks
  • Pull to one side, vibration, and uneven wear
  • Axle alignment and thrust angle checks

Diagnosis includes leak down tests, service and parking brake tests, stroke and lining checks, runout and balance checks, ride height set, and a road test.

Preventive Maintenance

Scheduled PM for trucks and trailers to cut breakdowns and extend component life. Intervals are set by mileage, hours, season, and duty cycle.

We document every service, set reminders, and keep clear inspection records so your units stay compliant and ready for work.

Scope & Tasks We Handle

  • Engine oil and filter, fuel and air filters
  • Transmission and differential fluid checks
  • Coolant strength and level checks, top up or exchange
  • Chassis and driveline greasing
  • Brake lining wear, slack adjuster stroke, air dryer service
  • Belts, hoses, clamps, and surge tank inspection
  • Wheel bearings and hub seals inspection
  • Lighting, batteries, and charging system checks
  • Tire pressure set, tread and torque checks
  • DPF status checks and parked regen support

Reporting includes digital PM sheets, next due reminders, and parts and labor logs for your records.

Tires & Wheel Service

Mobile and in-shop tire service for trucks and trailers. We handle repairs, replacements, mounting, balancing, and roadside calls.

We set correct pressure and torque, check tread and valve stems, and make sure every wheel is safe for load and season.

Scope & Issues We Handle

  • Puncture repair with plug and patch
  • Tire replacement for steer, drive, trailer, and super single
  • Mounting, balancing, and bead seating
  • Valve stem and core service
  • Wheel nut and stud replacement and torque check
  • Dual tire matching and spacing issues
  • Irregular wear, cupping, and shoulder wear checks
  • Roadside tire assistance

Diagnosis through tread depth and pattern checks, leak tests, rim and bead inspection, runout checks, torque verification, and pressure calibration.

On-Site & Breakdown Assist

24/7 mobile repair for trucks and trailers across Winnipeg and nearby areas. We come to you, fix what is safe at the roadside, and keep your downtime low.

Our service trucks carry scan tools, fittings, belts, hoses, fluids, and tire gear so most issues are solved on the spot. If a shop repair is safer, we help arrange a quick move.

Scope & Issues We Handle

  • No start or hard start (batteries, cables, starters)
  • Air leaks in lines, valves, or chambers
  • Coolant leaks and overheating
  • Belt or hose failures
  • Electrical faults and blown fuses
  • ABS or brake warnings
  • Fuel delivery problems and priming
  • DEF or DPF alerts and parked regen help
  • Tire change and puncture repair

Service area: Perimeter Hwy, Trans-Canada, Headingley, Oak Bluff, Selkirk, and Steinbach. Safety first on every call.
